About Me

My name is Jonathan Saravia, a passionate game developer and gameplay programmer who enjoys developing video games, creating gameplay experiences, and experimenting with new technology to make unique games.

My main interests are gameplay, AI, and physics engine programming. I have a passion for creating smooth and cohesive object movement. I enjoy working independently and in team situations to create games that immerse players into the game world.

I have been working with Game Development for over 6 years, developing games in Unity mainly as a game programmer. I have also spent thousands of hours gaining experience with gameplay mechanics.

I have attended Niagara College for Game Development and Conestoga College for Game Design.
